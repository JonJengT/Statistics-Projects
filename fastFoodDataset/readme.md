I created 3 visualizations for the fastfoodnutrition dataset.

I used R studio to create these visualizations. I got the dataset from my professor, which he got from Kaggle.com. I have also included the dataset in this file, titled, "fastfoodnutrition.csv".

Here are the questions for the following visualizations:
1: Sodium levels (in mg) of items per restaurant.
2: Comparison of chicken sandwiches to cheeseburgers. (Calories, Sodium, and Total Fat)
3: Sodium to serving size (concentration of sodium) per restaurant. (mg of sodium per gram of "food").
